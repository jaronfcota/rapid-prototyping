Apparently it could be a pretty good heat generator. Could potentially have a communal place where you and others could place your waste. Or it can be installed in an area with a lot of biomass like falling leaves that can just fall into the contraption. #### Renewable air compressor Use wind/solar to compress air that may be released to either fill tires or integrate into pneumatic systems or other things you can do with compressed air.
